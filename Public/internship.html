Internship

Data Analyst Intern

Company: Crescent Infotec, Erode

Duration: December 2023 - March 2024

Internship Description

As a Data Analyst Intern at Crescent Infotec, I was responsible for analyzing sales data to identify trends, patterns, and factors influencing performance. This involved using various data analysis tools and techniques to extract meaningful insights from large datasets.

Project: Sales Performance Analysis

The Sales Performance Analysis project focused on evaluating the sales data to understand the key drivers of sales performance. The main objectives of the project were:

  • Identify sales trends over different periods.
  • Analyze patterns in sales data to determine high and low-performing products.
  • Understand the factors influencing sales, such as seasonal variations, promotions, and market conditions.
  • Provide actionable insights and recommendations to improve sales performance.

Through this project, I gained valuable experience in data visualization, statistical analysis, and report generation, which significantly contributed to my professional growth.
